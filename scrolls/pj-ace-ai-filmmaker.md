## Workflow Stack
1. **Narrative Design** – Hooks, beat sheets, and satire angles co-written with LLMs (ChatGPT, Gemini, Grok) using reusable prompt templates for comedic pacing and platform fit.
2. **Visual Generation** – Veo 3 as the spine for text-to-video with reference alignment, native audio, and 3–5 iterations per shot; Kling used for specialty sequences.
3. **Reference & Style Control** – Midjourney V7 Omni Reference and Ideogram supply character/brand fidelity; omni-weight tuning sustains continuity across edits.
4. **Voice & Sound** – ElevenLabs powers cloned voices and dialogue sweetening; Veo audio stems cleaned in Final Cut Pro or CapCut.
5. **Edit & Delivery** – AI-assisted editing (Final Cut Pro, DaVinci Resolve, CapCut) for grading, masking, auto-captioning, and Smart Conform to vertical formats.
6. **Collaboration Layer** – Figma boards, Google Docs shotlists, and swarm-accessible prompt libraries keep distributed creatives aligned for rapid revisions.

## Operating Principles
- **Attention First** – Humor, shock, or meme energy leads before any brand CTA; audience retention metrics govern revisions.
- **Speed & Cost Compression** – Typical production runs ~$500 in Veo credits versus $500K legacy shoots, enabling multi-day turnaround.
- **Transparency as Growth Loop** – Shares behind-the-scenes threads, prompt packs, and newsletters (“Steal Our Formula”) to seed community adoption and recruit collaborators.
- **Platform Native Delivery** – Tailors edits for TikTok, Reels, X, and YouTube, reformatting aspect ratios, captions, and pacing per algorithmic norms.
